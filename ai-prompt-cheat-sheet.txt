========================================
   AI PROMPT CHEAT SHEET
   20+ Battle-Tested Prompts
   For Freelance Success
========================================

📌 PROMPT #1: Upwork Proposal That Wins

"Write a Upwork proposal for this job: [paste job description]. I have experience in [your skill]. Address the client's specific problem, offer a free sample, and end with a question. Keep it under 200 words. Sound confident."

📌 PROMPT #2: Blog Post Writer

"Write a blog post about [topic]. Target audience: [describe reader]. Tone: [friendly/expert/educational]. Length: 1500 words. Include: introduction, 3 main sections with subheadings, conclusion with call to action. Use short sentences and examples."

📌 PROMPT #3: Social Media Captions (30 days)

"Create 30 social media captions for [Instagram/Twitter/LinkedIn]. Topic: [your niche]. Each caption: under 200 characters, includes 1 emoji, ends with a question to boost engagement. Mix educational, entertaining, and promotional posts."

📌 PROMPT #4: Client Email (Follow-up)

"Write a follow-up email to a freelance client. I delivered [project name] 5 days ago. No response yet. Be polite, professional, and offer a small additional revision for free. Ask if they need anything else."

📌 PROMPT #5: Portfolio Case Study

"Write a case study for my portfolio. Project: [describe]. Problem the client had. Solution I provided. Results (use numbers: faster, cheaper, more sales). Keep it to 300 words. Sound results-driven."

📌 PROMPT #6: Cold Outreach Message

"Write a cold message to a business owner. I offer [your service]. Their business is [industry]. Offer one free suggestion to improve their [website/social media/sales copy]. End with low-pressure question. Keep it under 150 words."

📌 PROMPT #7: Fiverr Gig Description

"Write a Fiverr gig description for [your service]. Include: what I deliver, why choose me, what the client needs to provide, delivery time, and 3 FAQs. Sound professional and trustworthy. Use short bullet points."

📌 PROMPT #8: Email Newsletter

"Write a weekly newsletter for [industry/topic]. Subject line: [write 3 options]. Body: share 1 tip, 1 tool recommendation, 1 quick win. Add a personal story at the beginning. Keep it under 500 words. Friendly tone."

📌 PROMPT #9: Client Questionnaire

"Create a client onboarding questionnaire for a freelancer offering [your service]. Ask: project goals, target audience, brand voice examples, deadlines, budget range, and anything else they should share. 10 questions maximum."

📌 PROMPT #10: Rate Increase Message (Existing Client)

"Write a message to an existing client. I want to raise my rate from $[current] to $[new] per hour. Explain that I've delivered great results, have more experience now, and still offer priority support. Offer to keep current rate for 1 more month as transition."

========================================
   BONUS: Quick Setup For Best Results
========================================

✅ Always add: "Write in clear, simple English. Avoid fluff."
✅ Always add: "Use short paragraphs. Max 3 sentences per paragraph."
✅ For long content: "Think step by step before writing."
✅ For revisions: "Make it more [persuasive/funny/professional]."
